The challenge...

Imagine you have £1 million to lend, and several thousand people who want to borrow it. Your challenge is to devise a solution that helps ensure the right people get the money - in this case, "the right people" means those who are going to pay it back (with interest)!

We will provide you with a big lump of data on the (anonymous) applicants. For a subset of this data, we will also tell you the decision made on them by a real personal loans provider, and how well they're behaving as customers so far. We'll also give you some examples of real world loan application websites and decision systems.

Exactly how you tackle the challenge is up to you. If machine learning floats your boat, you may use your time to evolve an algorithm that works out which data points are the best predictors of future performance. Or perhaps you will build a plugin that augments the data with a rich vein of data scraped from social media platforms. If you are web or mobile app development fanatics, perhaps you will build a truly awesome application process or a world-class API. Or maybe data visualisation is your passion, and you'll develop the sweetest of dashboards to report on application stats and account performance.

The judges will assess each solution based on its own merits, but will be most impressed by solutions that demonstrate the best way to maximise return on the theoretical £1 million loan fund. You can still win first prize if the judges believe that your solution is genuinely innovative or well built, but can't quite prove itself against the data provided.
